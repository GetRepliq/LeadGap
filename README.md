**LeadGap** is an AI-driven market intelligence engine that bridges the gap between consumer frustration and local business solutions. By listening to the "digital exhaust" of public forums and reviews, LeadGap identifies real-world service voids, allowing contractors to stop guessing and start solving.

Instead of generic ad spend, LeadGap provides **high-fidelity lead guidance**: telling businesses exactly *what* the market is missing, *where* the demand is peaking, and *how* to phrase the solution.

## 🛠 How It Works

### 1\. Data Ingestion

The system employs a distributed scraping and API integration layer to harvest raw customer sentiment from Google Reviews (Reddit, Nextdoor, Yelp, and more to be added soon).

  * **Hyper-Local Scoping:** Data is filtered by geospatial parameters and industry-specific keywords (e.g., "HVAC," "Roofing," "Plumbing").
  * **Real-Time Monitoring:** Captures "in-the-moment" urgency—such as localized power outages or storm damage—ensuring insights are grounded in immediate buyer pain.

### 2\. Neural Signal Extraction & Intent Scoring

Raw text is pushed through a custom NLP pipeline to strip noise and normalize regional slang.

  * **Linguistic Signaling:** Our models identify specific markers of "unmet expectations" (e.g., *"No one called me back,"* or *"They don't service this brand"*).
  * **Intent Scoring:** Each signal is weighted using a proprietary **Purchase Intent Index**, distinguishing between general venting and high-urgency service needs.

### 3\. Service Gap Analysis (The "Opportunity Engine")

This is the core of LeadGap. The system cross-references identified pain points against current local competitor offerings.

  * **Mismatch Detection:** Identifies "blind spots" where demand is high but local advertising is silent or ineffective.
  * **Actionable Copywriting:** Generates data-backed ad hooks and sales scripts that mirror the exact language customers use when they feel ignored.

### 4\. Automated Lead Routing (Future Scope)

Aggregated insights are delivered via a dashboard or API, providing contractors with a "Heat Map" of local dissatisfaction, allowing for surgical precision in Google Ads and Meta campaigns.

## 📈 The LeadGap Impact

  * **For Contractors:** Lower Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) by targeting niche frustrations competitors are ignoring.
  * **For Consumers:** Faster access to services that actually solve their specific problems.
  * **For Marketers:** A move away from "creative intuition" toward "data-driven empathy."
